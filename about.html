Gaming

I've always been fascinated with technology and how it functions and it transfering from a machine to a screen producing scenery. Now the world is still producing fascinating tech like Apples Virtual Reality which shows tabs of your favorite streaming service float in your face and be interactive.

It's super cool and I'm looking forward for something that revolutionizes the world and helps it for the better.

Gaming is like basically like any other persons favorite activity to pass the time, an escape from reality! I'm now starting to go more into the IT route and I think it would be pretty cool to be a part of the web, leaving my markings and work along the way.

As anyone, being a part of something is a very good feeling in your core. I'm happy I got to grow up with the advancement of technology because I saw the massive progress and advances of tech. I'm also glad I haven't lost my love for gaming as I still am a heavy gamer. Streaming is something I am interested in as well but we'll see if life has room for me to do that haha.

Sports

I graduated from High School not to long ago and I was pretty active in sports. Anything doing with running got my attention and got me fired up. I enjoyed playing a variety of sports, games, or 5K run. My school had a tradition of running a 5K run around school campus and I loved being competitive against my friends.

Although I enjoy playing sports, for the life of me, I cannot play them at all. I will always try my best but when I know I drag the team down or make us have a disadvantage, I always look for other ways on helping.

Soccer and football were the sports I mainly played and a bit of batminton for a while. I liked to take pride in my foot work and ability to be hard to drop on the floor. I was and still am a stubborn guy when it comes to competitive sports.

I try my best to help others and give others a chance to shoot their shot and practice. I'm always aware of balance and I try my best to equal out the field when I could and at times take charge.
